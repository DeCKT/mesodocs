Meso MMO

A work-in-progress collection of documents created in the development of yet to be named Mesoamerican based MMORPG.

Introduction

Introduction

Runescape-like MMORPG with elements like skill training, social interactions, intriguing combat, equipment collection, fashion collection, and completionist activities.

Skills

There will be 4 categories of skills

  • Combat
  • Collection
  • Production
  • Support

Skills Start at level 1, and max out at level 60. Skills will have various ways to train, with easier methods providing less progress towards levels. Harder, more intensive methods provide quicker ways to level up a skill. At the most efficient methods, a skill should take about 100 hours to level up all the way.

Combat

Many other MMOs will utilize 3 forms of combat, being melee, ranged, and magic. Melee will focus on utilizing weapons such as swords. Ranged will focus on utilizing ranged weaponry like bows and arrows. Magic is unique in that instead of using physical weapons to deal damage, damage is done to opponents via magical spells. Many times magic will require some sort of resource, being magical runes, mana, or energy. This game, while not exactly non-fiction, is less based in fantasy, and so the available combat skills for a player to train will be melee and ranged.

The game Runescape, which this game is highly inspired by, provides what is called a combat triangle to help balance the differnt forms of combat. A warrior's armor is strong against the piercing ammunition from an archer, while especially weak against the powerful spells of a mage. Similarly, an archer's lightweight leather armor is easily shredded by the sharp tools of a warrior, while absorbing and counteracting the damage of a mage's spells. A mage's robes are not protective at all, and are easily pierced by an archer's arrows, bolts or knives. However, the advantage that a mage has is the ability to keep their distance from their warrior target.

The balance of this game is inspired by that of Runescape, however, with only 2 forms of combat, in order to be competetive a player must utilize both forms of combat.
